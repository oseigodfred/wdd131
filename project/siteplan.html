Website Site Plan

MindStrong Men

Site Name

MindStrong Men

This name represents strength, resilience, and mental wellness for men. It was chosen to encourage men to openly discuss mental health without stigma.

Optional domain: mindstrongmen.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to raise awareness about men’s mental health. It provides educational content, coping strategies, motivational messages, and access to support resources to help men manage stress, anxiety, and depression.

Scenarios

  • How can I recognize signs of stress or depression in myself?
  • Where can I find help or talk to someone about my mental health?
