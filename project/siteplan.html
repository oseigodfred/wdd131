Website Site Plan

MindStrong Men

Site Name

MindStrong Men

A name representing strength, resilience, and awareness for men’s mental health.

Domain: mindstrongmen.org

Site Purpose

The website raises awareness about men’s mental health by providing education, coping strategies, and support resources to help reduce stigma and encourage help-seeking.

Scenarios

  • How can I recognize signs of depression or anxiety in myself?
  • Where can I find help or speak to a professional about my mental health?
